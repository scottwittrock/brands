# Nimbus

**Tagline:** Calm software for busy teams.

Nimbus is a fictional cloud-productivity brand used as an example in this
registry. It makes calendaring, notes, and lightweight project tracking feel
quiet and effortless. The brand's job is to lower the temperature of work: fewer
alarms, clearer defaults, more trust.

## Personality

Nimbus is the composed colleague who has already read the thread and summarized
it for you. Precise without being cold. Reassuring without being fluffy.
Confident enough to hide complexity rather than show it off.

## At a glance

- **Category:** Cloud productivity (calendar, notes, tasks)
- **Audience:** Small-to-mid teams who feel over-tooled and under-focused
- **Feeling to leave behind:** "That was easier than I expected."
- **Three words:** Clear, calm, dependable
- **Not:** Loud, gimmicky, urgent
