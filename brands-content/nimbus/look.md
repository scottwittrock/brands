# Nimbus — Look

The Nimbus look is airy and precise: cool blues on generous white space, one
confident accent, and geometric type. When in doubt, add space, not decoration.

## Color

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Primary | Nimbus Blue | `#2f6bff` | Primary buttons, links, key emphasis |
| Ink | Slate Ink | `#0f1729` | Headlines and body text |
| Muted | Fog | `#5b6472` | Secondary text, captions |
| Surface | Mist | `#f4f7fb` | Cards, section backgrounds |
| Background | Paper | `#ffffff` | Page background |
| Border | Hairline | `#e2e8f2` | Dividers, input borders |

**Rules**

- Blue is a scalpel, not a highlighter — one primary action per view.
- Text is Slate Ink on white, never pure black on pure white.
- Backgrounds stay light; avoid dark UI except for code or media.

## Typography

- **Headings:** Space Grotesk — geometric, slightly technical, tight tracking.
- **Body:** Inter — neutral and highly legible at small sizes.
- **Scale:** 1.25 ratio. Headlines are large and calm; body is 16px minimum.
- Letter-spacing tightens on large headings (`-0.02em`), never on body.

## Logo & shape

- Wordmark set in Space Grotesk Medium, lowercase "nimbus".
- Corners are softly rounded (8–12px). Nothing sharp, nothing pill-shaped.
- Iconography is thin-stroke and rounded, 1.5px line weight.

## Space & layout

- Generous whitespace is the brand. Let sections breathe.
- Single-column reading measures; max line length ~70 characters.
- Motion is subtle: 150ms ease, small translate. Nothing bounces.

## Imagery

- Soft gradients from Mist to white; occasional cloud/sky motif, never literal.
- Product screenshots shown calm and uncluttered, real content over lorem ipsum.

## Do / Don't

- **Do** rely on space and hierarchy to guide the eye.
- **Do** keep one accent color doing the work.
- **Don't** stack multiple bright colors or heavy drop shadows.
- **Don't** use urgent reds or exclamation-heavy badges.
