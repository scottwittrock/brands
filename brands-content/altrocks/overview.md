# The Altrocks

**Tagline:** Five people, five colors, one torn-paper pile.

The Altrocks is a family brand for the household of Jovey, Lacey, Scott, Ivy, and
Rue (the dog), of Northern Liberties, Philadelphia. It dresses everyday family
life — cups, bins, backpacks, chore rows, door labels, holiday cards, Rue's tag —
in one hand-made torn-paper style that anyone in the house can make things in,
including the kids.

## Personality

The Altrocks is a stack of torn cardstock leaning cheerfully to one side. Warm,
tactile, and unfussy. It looks handmade because it is — big flat shapes ripped by
hand, no outlines, no gloss. Playful without being cute, organized without being
precious. The pile leans; that lean is the whole personality.
