# Cleo — Look

The Cleo look is soft, rounded, and warm: sage green and warm peach on a cream
background, generous white space, and friendly rounded type. It should feel like
early-morning light — calm and reassuring — never clinical, neon, or cluttered.

## Color

Core identity (parent brand / Cleo Track):

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Primary | Sage Green | `#a8c5a0` | Primary actions, the wordmark period, accents |
| Primary (dark) | Deep Sage | `#8ab082` | Hover / pressed states |
| Accent | Warm Peach | `#f4a97f` | Highlights, secondary emphasis, illustration |
| Text | Slate | `#3d4a52` | Headings and body text |
| Background | Cream | `#faf7f2` | Page background |
| Surface | Linen | `#f3f0eb` | Cards, sidebars, panels |
| Border | Warm Border | `#ddd4c8` | Dividers, card borders, inputs |

**Rules**

- Palettes stay warm and soft — no neon, no high-saturation colors.
- Sage green is the signature; warm peach supports it. Don't add competing hues.
- Text is Slate on cream, never pure black on pure white.

## Typography

A two-font system, shared across every Cleo app:

- **Display / headings:** **Nunito** — rounded and friendly; it carries the Cleo
  personality (titles, section headers, CTA buttons, the wordmark).
- **Body / UI:** **DM Sans** — clean and highly legible; keeps body copy readable
  without competing with the headings.
- Sentence case everywhere — never Title Case or ALL CAPS.

## Logo & wordmark

- The wordmark is **"Cleo."** — title case, bold Slate text, with a **sage-green
  period** (`#a8c5a0`), same weight and size as the preceding letter.
- The wordmark always uses **DM Sans** (`font-body font-bold`), not Nunito.
- Product names use title case: **Cleo Track**, **Cleo Routine**, **Cleo Cash**,
  **Cleo Plan**.
- Corners everywhere are softly rounded; nothing stiff, sharp, or clinical.

## Illustration & imagery

- Filled rounded shapes with soft internal shading, no hard outlines.
- Characters have dot eyes, curved mouths, and rosy cheek ellipses; shapes
  overlap and nest.
- Small and charming (sleepy baby, tiny moon, a tasteful poop emoji).
- **Avoid:** clipart, overly literal imagery, busy layouts, or anything that
  feels like a hospital form or a timer counting down.

## Space & layout

- Generous white space is the brand — never cluttered.
- Large tap targets, minimal reading; "designed for 3am."
- If a screen shows more than three pieces of information, simplify it.

## Do / Don't

- **Do** lean on sage + peach on cream, with lots of breathing room.
- **Do** keep everything rounded, warm, and calm.
- **Don't** use neon, high-saturation color, or hard outlines.
- **Don't** make it feel clinical, urgent, or medical.
