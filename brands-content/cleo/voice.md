# Cleo — Voice

Cleo sounds like a calm, funny best friend who has already been through it. It
reassures first, informs second, and never adds to a tired parent's stress. Warm,
playful, and concise — always on the parent's side.

## Principles

1. **Warm and reassuring.** Lead with encouragement. "You're doing great.
   Seriously."
2. **Playful, not silly.** A light touch is welcome. "Log it before you forget
   it (yes, the poop too)."
3. **Never judgmental.** Every family is different. "Every baby is different.
   Here's what yours is doing."
4. **Concise.** No long paragraphs anywhere in UI or marketing. "Fed at 2:14am.
   8 min. Left side."

## Tone by context

- **Onboarding:** warm and brief; get them logging in one tap.
- **Empty states:** encouraging, never blank. "No logs yet today — first feed of
  the day incoming! 🍼"
- **Celebrations:** delight in the mundane — streaks, milestones, first events.
- **Health topics:** supportive, then point to a pediatrician. Never diagnose.

## Vocabulary

- **Say:** log, track, you, your baby, doing great, when you're ready, first feed
- **Avoid:** medical jargon, clinical language, anxiety-inducing comparisons,
  overwhelming statistics, "expert" tone, condescension

## Grammar & mechanics

- **Sentence case** for all headings and buttons — never Title Case or ALL CAPS.
- **Second person** ("you", "your baby") — never third person or passive voice.
- **Action-oriented buttons:** "Log feeding," not "Submit."
- Short sentences. No long paragraphs in product or marketing.
- Emoji allowed sparingly: 🌙 💧 🍼 💩 ⏱️

## Examples

- **Empty state —** ✅ "No logs yet today — first feed of the day incoming! 🍼"
  ❌ "You have not recorded any feeding events for the current date."
- **Button —** ✅ "Log feeding" ❌ "Submit entry"
- **Reassurance —** ✅ "You're doing great. Seriously." ❌ "Your tracking
  compliance is below the recommended threshold."
- **Log summary —** ✅ "Fed at 2:14am. 8 min. Left side." ❌ "Feeding session
  successfully persisted to the database."
