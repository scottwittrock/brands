# Cleo

**Tagline:** A family of apps that help parents navigate life with little ones.

Cleo is a suite of calm, beautifully designed apps for parents and children.
Each app is focused and polished — delight comes from execution, not feature
count. The core promise across every product is the same: make the exhausting
chaos of new parenthood feel manageable — even a little joyful.

## Personality

Cleo is a calm, funny best friend who has already been through it. Warm and
reassuring, playful without being silly, and never judgmental. It meets
sleep-deprived parents where they are — offering steadiness and a laugh as much
as data. It never lectures, never induces anxiety, and never sounds like a
hospital form.

## The suite

- **Cleo Track** — feeding, sleep, and diaper tracking for new parents (iOS + web).
- **Cleo Routine** — a gentle, child-operated morning routine for toddlers 2–5 (iOS).
- **Cleo Cash** — money and chores for kids (iOS + Android).
- **Cleo Plan** — lightweight planning for families (web).

The parent brand is always **Cleo.** (with the sage-green period). The paid tier
for Cleo Track is **Cleo+** — never "Cleo Track+".

## At a glance

- **Category:** Family & parenting apps (tracking, routines, kids' money, planning)
- **Audience:** First-time parents — sleep-deprived, overwhelmed, looking for
  reassurance as much as data
- **Feeling to leave behind:** "That was easier than I expected — and kind of joyful."
- **Three words:** Calm, warm, effortless
- **Not:** Clinical, anxiety-inducing, cluttered, preachy

## What Cleo is not

- Not a medical tool — always points to a pediatrician for health concerns.
- Not a parenting-advice platform — it tracks and supports, it doesn't prescribe.
- Not overwhelming — if a screen has more than three pieces of information, it simplifies.
