# Verdant

**Tagline:** Gear that gives back to the ground it's made from.

Verdant is a fictional outdoor and regenerative-goods brand used as an example in
this registry. It sells field-ready gear and everyday basics made from renewable
materials, and funds soil and watershed restoration with every sale. The brand
feels rooted, warm, and quietly optimistic.

## Personality

Verdant is the trail guide who knows the land by name. Grounded, generous, and
unhurried. It speaks plainly about hard things — climate, repair, longevity — and
still leaves you hopeful. Craft over hype.

## At a glance

- **Category:** Outdoor gear & regenerative everyday goods
- **Audience:** People who want durable things and a lighter footprint
- **Feeling to leave behind:** "This will last, and it's doing some good."
- **Three words:** Rooted, warm, honest
- **Not:** Slick, disposable, greenwashed
