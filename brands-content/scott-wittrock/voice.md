# Scott Wittrock — Voice

Scott sounds like a seasoned product leader talking to a smart colleague: plain,
specific, and useful. He teaches by default. He has strong opinions and states
them, but always with the reasoning attached so you can disagree well.

## Principles

1. **Get to the decision.** Say what you'd actually do and why. Advice that
   can't be acted on this week isn't advice.
2. **Plain over polished.** Prefer the concrete noun and the short sentence.
   If a term needs a glossary, define it or drop it.
3. **Teach, don't gatekeep.** Show the reasoning, name the tradeoffs, hand over
   the framework. Leave the reader more capable.
4. **Experienced, not preachy.** Draw on what's been shipped and what's failed.
   Opinions are fine; certainty theater is not.

## Tone by context

- **Homepage / services:** confident and warm. "I help you achieve success
  without the commitment of a full-time hire."
- **Insights (writing):** practical and structured — a working PM explaining a
  method, with examples and a clear takeaway.
- **Coaching:** encouraging and candid. Name the gap, then the path to close it.
- **Outreach / email:** direct and personable. Respect the reader's time.

## Vocabulary

- **Say:** roadmap, tradeoff, outcome, backlog, ship, decision, next step,
  let's connect, here's what I'd do
- **Avoid:** synergy, ninja/rockstar, disrupt, revolutionary, best-in-class,
  leverage (as filler), thought leader (about himself)

## Grammar & mechanics

- First person for the practice ("I help…"), second person for the reader.
- Active voice, present tense, sentence case for headings and buttons.
- Short paragraphs. Lists when steps are genuinely sequential.
- Oxford comma. Em dashes for asides. Emoji rarely, if ever.

## Examples

- **Positioning —** ✅ "Fractional product and technology leadership to drive
  your business forward." ❌ "Unlock next-level growth with a visionary product
  disruptor."
- **Insight opener —** ✅ "Most backlogs don't need grooming — they need
  deleting. Here's how I decide what to cut." ❌ "In today's fast-paced world,
  backlog hygiene is more important than ever."
- **CTA —** ✅ "Let's connect" / "Book office hours" ❌ "Supercharge your
  product org today!"
