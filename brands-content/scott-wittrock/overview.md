# Scott Wittrock

**Tagline:** Head for Product. Product and technology leadership, on demand.

Scott Wittrock is a technical product leader with over a decade of experience
shipping software and building product teams. His personal brand operates under
the banner **Head for Product** — a fractional CPO/CTO practice, 1:1 coaching for
product managers, and a steady stream of practical writing on how product work
actually gets done.

The through-line is the same whether Scott is leading a team, coaching an
individual, or publishing an essay: strip away the jargon, get to the decision,
and leave people more capable than you found them.

## Personality

Scott is the experienced operator who has seen the failure modes and won't let
you walk into them. Direct without being blunt for its own sake. Generous with
what he knows — the coaching instinct shows up everywhere. He's technical enough
to earn engineering's trust and commercial enough to earn the CEO's, and he
translates fluently between the two.

## What the brand covers

- **Head for Product** — the practice: fractional product/technology leadership,
  1:1 coaching, and advisory work.
- **Insights** — writing on product management craft: backlogs, epics, kanban,
  story writing, product vision, roadmaps.
- **Scott Wittrock, the person** — the credible human behind the practice.
